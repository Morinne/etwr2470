Truth 1: We are Movement Machines

Our bodies are built to move, from the functioning of our eyes to the shape of our posture to size of our brains. Our ancestors moved all day long, and by adding in natural movement to our day, we can be true to what our bodies need.

Truth 2: We are Mind Magicians

Your brain is an incredibly complex organ, controlling your movements, your emotions, and your most basic bodily functions. So much of what we do and think is run on auto-pilot, with our brain at the helm. By learning all we can about the brain, we can take over the driver’s seat instead of going along for the ride in the passenger seat.

Truth 3: We are Nourished by Nature

Even before our trusty canine companion joined forces with us 35,000 years ago, nature has been with us. It was our first home, where we grew up as a species surrounded by fresh air, big skies, and vibrant greenery. Its touch influences many of our biological processes, so spending time in our first home is essential.

Truth 4: We are Eaters of Everything*

Many nutrition gurus will lead you to believe that there is one single ideal diet, but the truth is, there wasn’t a single human diet even back in paleolithic times. Humans eat everything from seals to grasshoppers to tofu, not just sampling different foods, but thriving on them. *EXCEPT, we are each genetically unique and have unique requirements and limitations. Learn to listen to your own body and find your personal food truths
